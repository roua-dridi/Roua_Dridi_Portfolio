Piafa – Mobile Application for Women's Health and Cycle Tracking

Piafa is a mobile application developed with Flutter that empowers women to understand and monitor their menstrual health across three key life phases: pre-puberty, puberty, and menopause. The app features cycle tracking, symptom logging, pregnancy and ovulation prediction, and educational content in the form of articles and videos. It also includes separate interfaces for women, doctors (for content contribution and Q&A), and administrators. Piafa combines user-friendly design with real-time data insights to promote awareness, health education, and proactive well-being.

  • Flutter, Next.js, Nest.js, GraphQL, PostgreSQL, Prisma
MediTimeaid

MediTimeAid is a comprehensive Flutter-based mobile application for managing medical appointments and care for elderly patients. Users can easily book and track appointments, receive reminders, and maintain a personalized health record. Doctors have dedicated accounts that allow them to manage their schedules, consult patient records, and send tailored reminders or updates to their patients. Additionally, le responsable de suivi (caregiver for the elderly) has a special role in MediTimeAid, allowing them to monitor health updates, coordinate care, and ensure that elderly patients never miss an important appointment. All accounts are interconnected within MediTimeAid, ensuring smooth communication and streamlined care between doctors, patients, and caregivers. This synergy enhances the overall healthcare experience and promotes a collaborative approach to health management.

  • Flutter, Flask, Firebase, elasticSearch
Le Portail

Municipal Portal is a mobile and web application designed to streamline communication between citizens and their municipality. It allows residents to easily submit complaints, sign petitions, request access to public information, and stay updated with the latest news and announcements published by the municipality. Citizens can also track the status of their submissions in real-time, while municipal staff can efficiently manage and respond to requests, ensuring transparent, responsive service for the entire community.

  • Flutter, .Net
Jeunesse et sport

Youth & Sports App is a mobile and web application divided into three main modules. The first module is designed for parents who wish to register their children in municipal kindergartens. Parents can submit a registration form directly through the app and receive the application status and updates. The second module is for athletes and sports enthusiasts looking to rent a sports field or a swimming lane. They can easily make bookings through the app and receive a QR code as confirmation. The third module is dedicated to sports facility managers, allowing them to verify bookings and grant access by scanning QR codes at the entrance. This streamlined system simplifies registrations, bookings, and access control, ensuring a smooth and efficient experience for all users.

  • Flutter, .Net
Le Garage

Le Garage is a mobile and web application owned and operated by a specific car garage. It allows customers to browse cars available for sale — each with a detailed description — and easily schedule maintenance services offered by the garage, including painting, brake repair, inspections, and battery replacement. Users can track the progress of their vehicle’s repair work in real time, and access dedicated technical support to submit complaints or ask questions. The app provides a streamlined, transparent, and personalized experience for all of the garage’s customers.

  • Flutter, .Net
